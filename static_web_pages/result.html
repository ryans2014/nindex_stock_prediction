FAQ:
Q - What is 20-day prediction exactly?
Prediction of the average daily closing price in future 20 trading days. The price is represented by percentage based on the most recent closing price.
Q - How are these number predicted?
These predictions are based on numerical models trained by the past 15 years of SP500 stock data. Check model description for details.
Q - What does the red and green color region mean in the figure?
These represent historical predictions with this numerical model. To make a prediction for a day in the past, only data before that day is used. A positive 20-day prediction is representd by green while a negative prediction is represented by red. The scale of the green/red region means the predicted price change of the future 20-day average closing price.
